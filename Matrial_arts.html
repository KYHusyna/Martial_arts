Mixed martial arts: represent a combination of many techniques, schools and targeted martial arts

MMA (Mixed martial arts) are full-contact fighting with the use of striking techniques and wrestling in a standing position (clinch), and on the floor (parterre).

The origins of MMA go back to BC: even ancient Greeks competed at the first Olympic Games in Pankration, however, until the 1990s, MMA didn't have much sensibility in the world.

There are 2 opponents in the ring and 1 judge who monitors compliance with the rules. There are several types of rounds, 5 rounds of 3 minutes or 3 rounds of 5 minutes.
